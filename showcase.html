Crimson Moon

Crimson Moon is a fantasy RPG rogue-lite wherein the player steps into the shoes of a girl who is having her first period and enters the fantasy dreamscape where period symptoms are monstrous enemies, each representing different symptoms related to the menstrual cycle. "Leaks", a relatively harmless enemy, symbolises the panic and annoyance of being unprepared or just unlucky and bleeding through one's clothes. The next enemies are Nausea and Cramps. The final boss of the game represents a very different type of "symptom": Shame. An amalgamation of the stigma, humiliation, embarrassment, misinformation, and ignorance girls and women face surrounding their periods. Shame is given life and power by Shame's Helpers - all those who aid in continuing the existence of shame surrounding periods.

Pilot Action-Narrative RPG

Early concept art for a choice-based action and combat pilot sim RPG where the player is a citizen in a dystopic country chosen to become an elite pilot and compete in performance acrobatic jet-racing, or 'PEJ'. A beloved sport amongst the citizens, however, the government is secretly training these pilots for something far more sinister.
